About Me

I grew up in rural northern New Hampshire in the midst of the White Mountains. Growing up, I enjoyed many outdoor activities such as hiking, swimming, snowboarding, and snowmobiling. In school, I played basketball and ran cross country and track. At the age of 18, fresh out of high school, I moved to Los Angeles to fulfill a dream of mine; to do full time charity work. I volunteered full time for a non-profit called The Dream Center for three years. During that time I met my wife, Leah, who was also a volunteer at The Dream Center. In 2011, Leah and I moved to Asheville, NC and got married. We lived there for three years and enjoyed our time there immensely. Asheville is amazing! In 2015, we moved back to Los Angeles where we accepted full time paid positions at the same non-profit, The Dream Center. Currently, I am enrolled at The Coding Boot Camp at UCLA Extension. My goal is to become a full time web developer. Some of my more current hobbies include playing guitar, computers and tech, audio, disc golf, and craft beer.
